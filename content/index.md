# Daily Fantasy Sports Strategy and Research

Welcome to my knowledge base about daily fantasy sports.

This site will explore the research, strategy, and decision-making involved in building competitive DFS lineups. Topics will include player projections, ownership, roster construction, stacking, correlation, contest selection, late swap, bankroll management, and the analysis of winning lineups.

My goal is to organize useful DFS concepts and research into a resource that can grow throughout the semester.
