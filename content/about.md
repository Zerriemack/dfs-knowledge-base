# About

My name is Zerrie Mack, and I am passionate about football, data, and daily fantasy sports research.

I created this knowledge base to organize what I learn about DFS strategy and to document the concepts that shape strong lineup construction. I am especially interested in historical winning lineups, player usage, ownership, stacking, correlation, contest structure, and the differences between slate types.

I am also developing Milly Maker Tracker, a daily fantasy research platform focused on studying winning lineups and helping users better understand DFS strategy.
